Knifty Knights

Perfectly pocket-sized pixel people for your personal pleasure

Hail and well met traveller!

Welcome to Knifty Knights! A randomly generated collection of NFT's minted on the Polygon network and sold through Opensea.

Knifty Knights' primary purpose is an attempt to address the major concerns over an obscene under saturation of pocket-sized pixel people on the internet, by creating the best parts of what we believe any fantasy character should wear and then packaging each part up arbitrarily into the wonderful scattergun sausages you see before you.

So sit back, brandish your weapon of choice and discover your pixel alter ego.

A Knight for every taste

Each Knifty Knight comes with an array of layers of items to choose from, along with a randomly generated class and set of stats.

There are no rules in this game.You could be a sword wielding Wizard, or a Barbarian carrying a spellbook wearing a dress. You might even be dropped into the forest in your pants and a pint of ale!

Rares

Whilst all Knifty Knights are as special as the other, there does happen to be some that are yet a little more special, and a little more rare.

There are a number of rare, super rare, ultra rare, legendary and impossible items for you to collect through your adventures. Included in this are:

  • Flaming Weapons and clothing
  • Glacial Weapons and clothing
  • Mystical Staves
  • Dark Weapons
  • Auras
  • ...And more!

Not just limited to the glowing and glamourous, some characters may share some rare familiar traits from the sum of their parts that might appeal.

FAQS

Knifty Knights are randomly generated images minted into NFT's (Non-Fungible Token) on the Polygon network through Opensea

The purchase of any Knifty Knights allows unique ownership over that token, and the ability to hold and cherish for years to come, or sell on for your desired price.

To purchase a Knight Knight, log into your Metamask wallet and mint away! You can also visit the currently minted collection over on Opensea.

Polygon allows everybody to trade without having to worry about exorbitant gas fees, meaning you get much more for your money!

Minting spawns a brand new, unique Knifty Knight that has yet to be publically valued. It could have rare features that you can either gaze in awe at, or make a profit.
